# AI Systems Hub

## Project Overview

AI Systems Hub is an AI automation platform that builds intelligent voice receptionists for local businesses.

The first production product is an AI receptionist for automotive repair shops.

The receptionist answers incoming phone calls, understands customer requests, provides information, schedules appointments, and automates administrative work that would normally require a human receptionist.

The system is designed to operate with minimal human intervention while allowing seamless transfer to staff whenever necessary.

# Current Product

AI Auto Repair Receptionist

Capabilities:

- Answers inbound phone calls
- Understands natural conversation
- Identifies requested service
- Collects vehicle information
- Collects customer information
- Looks up services
- Gives approximate starting prices
- Estimates repair duration
- Checks calendar availability
- Books appointments
- Reschedules appointments
- Cancels appointments
- Sends confirmation SMS
- Sends cancellation SMS
- Sends reminder SMS
- Transfers calls to shop staff
- Takes messages
- Updates customer records
- Creates appointment records
- Stores conversation summaries

# Design Principles

The AI should always:

- Sound natural
- Be concise
- Ask only necessary questions
- Avoid hallucinations
- Never invent pricing
- Never invent availability
- Confirm important information
- Fail safely
- Escalate uncertainty to humans
