# AI Systems Hub

## Overview

AI Systems Hub is an AI automation platform for local businesses.

The first product is an AI-powered phone receptionist for automotive repair shops that automates customer communication and appointment management.

Current capabilities include:

- Answering inbound phone calls 24/7

- Identifying customer needs

- Collecting vehicle and customer information

- Answering frequently asked questions (business hours, services, and pricing)

- Looking up services from the shop's knowledge base

- Providing starting ballpark price estimates when applicable

- Estimating service duration

- Booking appointments

- Rescheduling existing appointments

- Cancelling appointments

- Sending appointment confirmations and notifications

- Capturing caller notes for follow-up after each call


## Project Goals

- Build the best AI receptionist for automotive repair shops.

- Deliver human-like customer conversations.

- Eliminate hallucinations.

- Improve booking accuracy.

- Provide reliable ballpark estimates.

- Automate appointment scheduling, rescheduling, and cancellations.

- Reduce response latency.

- Continuously improve through automated testing and AI-driven code reviews.

- Build an internal AI engineering platform capable of developing, testing, and optimizing AI agents.

Additional capabilities:

- Updating business records through automated workflows

- Integrating with Google Sheets and other business systems
